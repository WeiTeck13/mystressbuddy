Food for stress relieve

Some people eat more when they are feeling stress. But the eating habit can decide your health, so do avoid unhealthy eating. Here we will introduce you the food that can help relieving your stress, do try them out if you like it.

1. Dark chocolate

Rich in antioxidants, help reduce stress by reducing the stress hormones in the body.

2. Milk

Have relaxing effect on the body.

3. Tea

Drinking tea can calm your mind down.

4. Strawberry

Rich in vitamin C.
